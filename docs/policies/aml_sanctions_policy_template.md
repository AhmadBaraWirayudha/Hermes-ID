# AML and Sanctions Policy Template

This policy is a placeholder for future regulated financial activity. It may not apply if Hermes Analytics ID remains a general analytics and education platform without onboarding, payments, account opening, asset custody, or transaction execution.

## Trigger conditions

AML and sanctions controls must be reviewed if the platform adds:

- user onboarding for financial transactions
- payments or wallets
- brokerage connectivity
- asset transfers
- regulated financial product distribution
- crypto asset activity

## Future controls

- KYC and customer due diligence
- sanctions screening
- politically exposed person screening
- transaction monitoring
- suspicious activity escalation
- recordkeeping
- staff training
