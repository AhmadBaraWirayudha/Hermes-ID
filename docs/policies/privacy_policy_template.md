# Privacy Policy Template

## Purpose

This policy explains how Hermes Analytics ID collects, uses, stores, protects, and deletes personal data.

## Scope

Applies to users, administrators, analysts, partners, and visitors whose personal data is processed by the platform.

## Principles

- Lawful, fair, and transparent processing.
- Purpose limitation.
- Data minimization.
- Accuracy.
- Storage limitation.
- Integrity and confidentiality.
- Accountability.

## Personal data categories

Potential categories may include:

- account identity data
- contact data
- login and device data
- portfolio preference data
- risk profile answers
- support communications
- usage analytics

## Data subject rights

The operating process should support access, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent where applicable, objection, restriction, and complaint handling in line with applicable Indonesian law.

## Cross-border transfer

Any cross-border transfer must be reviewed under applicable Indonesian personal data protection requirements and supported by safeguards.

## Breach response

Potential personal data incidents must follow the incident response runbook and applicable notification requirements.
