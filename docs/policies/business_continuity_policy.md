# Business Continuity Policy

## Purpose

Maintain availability of critical analytics, data, alerts, and reports.

## Minimum controls

- daily database backup for production
- monthly restore drill
- health checks for frontend and API
- documented recovery runbook
- alternative manual CSV import process
- source failure handling
- incident communication procedure

## Recovery objectives

Starter targets:

- RPO for local MVP: 24 hours
- RTO for local MVP: 4 hours
- RPO for small production: 1 hour
- RTO for small production: 1 hour
