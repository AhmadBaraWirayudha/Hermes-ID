# AI Governance Policy

## Purpose

Ensure responsible AI use in market analytics, commentary support, forecasting, sentiment analysis, and robo-advisory workflows.

## Controls

- Human oversight for financial interpretation.
- Clear disclaimers for AI-generated outputs.
- Backtesting and validation for predictive models.
- Bias and fairness review for user risk profiling.
- Audit logs for model runs where feasible.
- No unsupported promise of returns.

## Prohibited claims

- guaranteed profits
- risk-free investing
- certain prediction of market movements
- regulatory approval unless formally obtained
