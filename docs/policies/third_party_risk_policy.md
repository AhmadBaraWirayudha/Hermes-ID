# Third-Party Risk Policy

## Scope

Applies to cloud providers, data providers, analytics vendors, open-source dependencies, hosting providers, and contractors.

## Due diligence checklist

- legal entity and contact details
- service description
- data processed
- location of processing
- security controls
- privacy terms
- uptime commitments
- exit plan
- incident notification terms
- subcontractor list where applicable

## High-risk vendors

A vendor is high risk if it processes personal data, regulated financial data, credentials, trading data, payment data, or production infrastructure.
