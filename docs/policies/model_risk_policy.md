# Model Risk Policy

## Scope

Applies to forecasts, anomaly detection, trend predictions, scenario analysis, and robo-advisory style decision support.

## Principles

- Models must be registered.
- Models must be backtested before production use.
- Model metrics must be stored.
- Users must be told that forecasts are uncertain.
- High-impact decisions require human review.
- Model drift must be monitored.

## Minimum evidence

- model registry entry
- training data window
- backtest results
- known limitations
- owner
- approval date
