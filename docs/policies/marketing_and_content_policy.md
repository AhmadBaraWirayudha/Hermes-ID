# Marketing and Content Policy

## Purpose

Prevent misleading statements and ensure financial content is accurate, balanced, and appropriate.

## Requirements

- Do not guarantee returns.
- Clearly distinguish education, research, opinion, and personalized advice.
- Disclose conflicts of interest.
- Use current and verified data sources.
- Include risk disclosures where market performance is discussed.
- Review influencer and affiliate campaigns for compliance risks.

## Content categories

- educational content
- market commentary
- regulatory explainers
- AI-assisted insights
- product marketing
