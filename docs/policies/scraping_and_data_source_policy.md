# Scraping and Data Source Policy

## Purpose

Define acceptable use of scraping, public data, APIs, CSV imports, and third-party data.

## Requirements

- Prefer official APIs and downloadable files.
- Review terms of service before scraping.
- Respect robots.txt where applicable.
- Use rate limits and delays.
- Do not bypass authentication, paywalls, technical controls, or access restrictions.
- Do not collect personal data unless approved by privacy review.
- Maintain source attribution.
- Disable a source immediately if a provider objects or terms change.

## Prohibited activity

- credential stuffing
- CAPTCHA bypass
- unauthorized redistribution of licensed data
- excessive request rates
- scraping private user data
- collection of sensitive personal data without approved legal basis
